My Weighted Calisthenics Journey

Hello there! I'm Abeyesolome Assefa, and I want to share my journey into weighted calisthenics with you.

My passion for weighted calisthenics ignited during the summer when I was studying abroad. The journey started in Prague, and the historic city became the backdrop for my fitness journey. I embraced the challenge with enthusiasm and dedication.

Weighted calisthenics has a rich history, blending bodyweight exercises with added resistance. It's not just a workout; it's a testament to strength, discipline, and pushing personal limits.

As I delved into this form of exercise, I discovered the roots of calisthenics trace back through time, drawing inspiration from ancient Greek and Roman training methods. Today, it has evolved into a modern fitness discipline that combines strength and aesthetics.

My days were filled with pull-ups, dips, and push-ups, each movement carrying the added challenge of external resistance. The journey wasn't just physical; it was a mental and emotional commitment to becoming the best version of myself.

Join me as I reflect on the history of weighted calisthenics and share the joy it has brought to my life. Here's to strength, growth, and the love of the journey!
